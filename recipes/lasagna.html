Lasagna

Descsription

An Italian staple consisting of layers of cheese, bolognese, and lasagna sheets

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Tomatoes
  • Italian herbs
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mozarella, ricotta, and parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna sheets
  2. Turn beef mince, tomatoes, and italians herbs into bolognese
  3. Grate and mix cheeses
  4. Layer the ingredients in a baking tray
  5. Bake in the oven
